# Volunteer Portal Launch Email

**To:** HMC Volunteer List (approx. 800 recipients)
**Subject:** Your HMC Volunteer Portal is ready — here's how to get started
**From:** Erica Robinson, Health Matters Clinic
**Reply-To:** volunteers@healthmatters.clinic

---

Dear [First Name],

Thank you for being part of Health Matters Clinic's volunteer community. What you do — whether you've served at one event or many — makes a real difference in the lives of people in Los Angeles who need it most.

We've been working hard behind the scenes to build something that serves you better. Today, we're officially launching the HMC Volunteer Portal — a dedicated home for everything related to your volunteer experience with us.

**What the portal gives you:**

- Your volunteer hours, tracked and always accessible
- Training modules and orientation resources, available on your own schedule
- Shift sign-ups and event registration in one place
- Direct communication with the HMC coordination team
- Upcoming events, calendars, and community resources
- The HMC Referral Hub, so you can connect people in your community to the services they need

This is not just an administrative tool. It is a way for us to stay connected, keep you informed, and make sure your time with HMC is well-supported and meaningful.

---

**Getting started is simple. Here is how:**

**Step 1 — Create your account**

Go to: https://volunteer.healthmatters.clinic

Click "Get Started" and sign up with your email address (or use Google sign-in for a faster setup). This takes less than two minutes.

**Step 2 — Complete your profile**

Once inside, fill out your volunteer profile. This helps us match you with the right opportunities, track your hours accurately, and reach you with events relevant to your skills and availability.

**Step 3 — Explore your tools**

Browse the Training Academy, check the calendar for upcoming events, and explore the Referral Hub. If you have not completed your HMC orientation yet, you can do that directly inside the portal.

---

**Not sure if you qualify as a volunteer? You're still welcome.**

Even if you are not currently an active HMC volunteer, we have a place for you. Anyone who creates an account will receive HMC Champion access — a community ambassador role that gives you access to our Resource Hub and Referral Hub, so you can share HMC resources with the people around you. If and when you want to step into a full volunteer role, you can apply directly through the portal.
