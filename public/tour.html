How It Works

Up and running
in under 5 minutes.

New or returning — the portal works the same way. No app download needed, works from any browser.

01

Create your account

Go to volunteer.healthmatters.clinic and sign up with Google or your email. It takes under two minutes — no app download, no credit card required.

volunteer.healthmatters.clinic
02

Complete your Training Academy modules

Self-paced orientation, mental health literacy, and community training — work through them on your own schedule. Finishing Tier 2 unlocks event sign-ups and expands your access to portal tools.

03

Sign up for a May event

Three Take Action LA events are happening in May — a morning walk, a community wellness meetup, and a live virtual session. Register directly from your portal dashboard.

04

Add it to your home screen

The portal is a full progressive web app — tap "Add to Home Screen" on iOS or Android for one-tap access anytime. No App Store required.
