Getting Started

You can be set up in under 5 minutes.

No app download required. The portal works on any device from your browser.

01

Create your account

Go to volunteer.healthmatters.clinic and sign up with your email or Google account. It takes less than two minutes. No credit card or app download needed.

volunteer.healthmatters.clinic
02

Complete your profile

Tell us about your skills, availability, and how you want to serve. This helps us match you with the right opportunities and track your contributions accurately.

03

Explore your tools

Browse the Training Academy, sign up for a shift, check the community calendar, or explore the Referral Hub to start connecting people to the services they need.

04

Add it to your home screen

The portal works as a full app on iOS and Android. From your browser, tap "Add to Home Screen" for instant access — no App Store required.

HMC Champion

Support the mission.
Earn rewards. Make an impact.

The HMC Champion role is for community members who want to show up for LA — sharing resources, attending events, and connecting people to the services they need. Champions earn points, unlock rewards, and are the backbone of HMC's community outreach.

Earn XP and Level Up

Every action earns you XP — sharing a resource, attending an event, making a referral. Level up from Champion to higher tiers and unlock more recognition along the way.

Your Personal Referral Link

Get a unique HMC link to share anywhere. Every person who joins through your link counts toward your impact score and earns you bonus points and recognition.

Prizes and Merch

Top Champions are recognized with HMC merch, event prizes, and early access to HMC experiences — including Take Action LA events and future programs.

Resource and Referral Hub

Browse 1,000+ free LA County services and connect people directly to mental health, housing, food, and more — right from your phone.

Community Events Access

Register for all HMC events — including the May Take Action LA series — directly through the portal. Champions always have a seat at the table.

Track Your Community Impact

Your dashboard shows your referrals made, events attended, and resources shared. Your impact is visible, counted, and celebrated — every action matters.

How It Works

Your link. Your community. Your impact.

When you create your account, you get a unique Champion link — something like volunteer.healthmatters.clinic?ref=yourname. Share it anywhere: Instagram, text, in person. Every person who joins through your link is credited to you.

1

Create your account and get your personal referral link

2

Share it — in person, on social, or by text — with anyone who could benefit from HMC resources

3

Earn XP and recognition each time someone joins through your link

4

Top referrers are highlighted as Community Leaders and get early access to prizes and events
