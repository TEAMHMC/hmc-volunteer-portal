The Unstoppable Workshop, Walks, Meetups, and Experience are four ways HMC is closing the mental health gap in our communities.

Scroll

The more we move, talk, and show up for each other, the more our communities heal.

The more we move, the more we heal.

Across Los Angeles, Black and underserved communities still carry the heaviest mental health burdens with the fewest culturally relevant places to turn. Unstoppable closes that gap with healing-centered spaces built by and for the people who need them most.

A grassroots model with citywide reach.

HMC's Unstoppable initiative brings together thousands of Angelenos through workshops, community walks, wellness meetups, and the Unstoppable Experience podcast, proving that movement, conversation, and showing up together changes outcomes.

The
Workshop

The Power of Healing & Growth Workshop is HMC's flagship in-person curriculum. Five modules. One day. A LACDMH-recognized program that moves participants from "I don't talk about this" to "I'm ready to keep talking."

5-module healing-centered curriculum Hosted at community centers and partner clinics Free for residents of LA County SPA 6

The
Report

HMC commissioned the Unstoppable evaluation to measure what happens when healing-centered programming reaches Black and underserved communities. LACDMH-validated outcomes from our first workshop cohorts:

28→69%Comfort discussing mental health
15→57%Aware of local resources
21→58%Know how to support a peer
22→53%Confident seeking care
