Mission

The more we move, the more we heal.

Yet Black and underserved Angelenos still face deep stigma, distrust, and barriers to mental health that compound across a lifetime.

Health Matters Clinic's flagship Unstoppable program, built with and for the community, explores the cultural realities that shape mental health and the grassroots support that drives healing.

Making a Difference

Across Los Angeles, community members, healers, and partners are already breaking the cycle of mental health stigma in Black and underserved neighborhoods.

FAQ

Here you will find the most commonly asked questions about Unstoppable. Every format on this page came from what people told us in workshops, walks, and street interviews.

The Workshop
What is the Workshop?

The Workshop is HMC's flagship in-person curriculum, recognized by LACDMH. Five healing-centered modules delivered in a single day, designed to move participants from "I do not talk about this" to "I am ready to keep talking."

Hosted at community centers and partner clinics across LA County SPA 6, free for residents.

How was it built?

The curriculum was developed with mental health champions, clinicians, and community members who told us what was missing from existing programs: cultural relevance, peer voices, and time to actually feel something.

Who is it for?

Adults and older youth in Black and underserved communities across LA County, especially residents of SPA 6, who want a healing-centered space without clinical gatekeeping.

How do I bring it to my community?

Partner orgs can request the Workshop through the HMC Partner Portal. Start a conversation at hello@healthmatters.clinic.

Live Unstoppable
What happens at a walk or run?

A community meet-up that turns movement into medicine. Neighbors, certified mental health champions, and Crenshaw runners gather, warm up, and move at any pace.

Do I need to be a runner?

No. Live Unstoppable is open to all ages and paces. Walkers, joggers, runners, strollers, and folks with mobility aids are all welcome. The route always has a walk-friendly version.

How often does it happen?

Monthly on the second Saturday at 8:00 AM, with routes through Crenshaw, Leimert Park, and South LA. Check the Event Finder for the next date and location.

How do I RSVP?

RSVP through the HMC Event Finder. Free and open to all.

Wellness Meetups
What is a Wellness Meetup?

Smaller, themed gatherings co-hosted with partner organizations. Topics range from mindfulness and grief circles to creative wellness and faith-rooted healing conversations.

Who hosts them?

HMC mental health champions and partner facilitators. Each meetup centers a culturally rooted approach to a specific wellness theme.

Are they free?

Yes. All Wellness Meetups are free and open to the public unless a partner-hosted event notes otherwise.

How do I co-host one?

Partner organizations can apply through the HMC Partner Portal to co-host a Wellness Meetup.

The Unstoppable Experience
What is the Experience?

A virtual podcast and panel series spotlighting the artists, athletes, clinicians, and everyday people who make their healing public.

Where can I watch?

Watch episodes on YouTube and listen on major podcast platforms.

Can I be a guest?

Yes. Share your story through the form above or reach out at hello@healthmatters.clinic.

How often are new episodes released?

New episodes are released monthly, with bonus drops around major mental health observances.
