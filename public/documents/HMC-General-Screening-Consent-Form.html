Health Matters Clinic

General Screening Consent Form

Clinician Acknowledgment Copy | Version 1.0

Purpose

This consent form is used for all community health screening events conducted by Health Matters Clinic. It authorizes volunteer clinicians to perform basic health screenings and provides participants with information about the services being offered.

As a Licensed Medical Professional, you must ensure that every participant signs this form (or its equivalent) before receiving any clinical services. This document serves as your acknowledgment of the consent process and your responsibilities in obtaining informed consent.

Services Covered

The General Screening Consent Form authorizes the following services:

  • Blood pressure measurement
  • Blood glucose testing (finger-stick capillary sample)
  • Body mass index (BMI) calculation
  • Health risk assessment questionnaire
  • Basic health education and resource referrals
  • HIV rapid testing (OraQuick) — when available and with separate pre/post-test counseling
  • Naloxone distribution and education — when available

Participant Rights

Participants have the right to:

  • Refuse any or all screenings at any time without consequence
  • Ask questions about any procedure before it is performed
  • Receive results of their screenings immediately
  • Receive referrals to follow-up care for any concerning findings
  • Have their health information kept confidential
  • Request services in their preferred language

Clinician Responsibilities

When obtaining consent, you must:

  • Explain the purpose of each screening in plain language
  • Describe any risks (e.g., minor discomfort from finger-stick, bruising from blood pressure cuff)
  • Confirm the participant understands and consents voluntarily
  • For minors: obtain consent from parent/legal guardian AND assent from the minor when appropriate
  • Provide a copy of the form to the participant if requested
  • Never coerce or pressure anyone into receiving services

Consent Form Template (Participant Version)

I voluntarily consent to receive free health screening services from Health Matters Clinic volunteers.

I understand that:

  • These screenings are for informational purposes only and do not replace regular medical care
  • The volunteers are licensed healthcare professionals donating their time
  • My information will be kept confidential and used only for providing services and follow-up referrals
  • I may refuse any screening at any time
  • Abnormal results will be explained to me with referral options for follow-up care

Important: Physical copies of signed consent forms must be collected at each event and returned to the Clinical Coordinator. Do not photograph or digitally store participant consent forms on personal devices.

Acknowledgment

By signing this document, you acknowledge that you understand the informed consent process, your responsibilities in obtaining participant consent, and your obligation to ensure proper handling of all consent documentation during Health Matters Clinic events.
